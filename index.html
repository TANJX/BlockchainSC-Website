Welcome to Blockchain SC

Blockchain SC is a university-based eco-system for blockchain. The organization specializes in facilitating innovating projects and discussion, and helping companies benefit from blockchain technology by identifying use cases, building out prototypes, and integrating solutions.

Whereas most technologies tend to automate workers on the periphery doing menial tasks, blockchains automate away the center. Instead of putting the taxi driver out of a job, blockchain puts Uber out of a job and lets the taxi drivers work with the customer directly.

Vitalik Buterin
